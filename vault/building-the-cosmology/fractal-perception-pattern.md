# The Fractal Perception Pattern

## Purpose

Defines the single perception shape that repeats at every scale of the story, so a reader can recognize meaning without being told it.

## Core Principle

Partial perception → recognition → incomplete interpretation → deeper pattern.

## Explanation

This shape isn't unique to any one character or scene — it applies to characters seeing each other incompletely, relationships beginning through recognition before understanding, the reader seeing fragments and building meaning gradually, Unity exposing connected fragments while still being perceived locally, and Qalm itself tracking outcomes without ever experiencing totality all at once.

The story should never give total explanation first. It should let meaning appear through partial evidence, recognition, and later re-reading — the same shape, repeating, until it becomes visible as a pattern rather than a coincidence.
