# Kael as Anomaly

## Purpose

Defines what breaks the Continuity Cycle — the one fragment the system can no longer classify or resolve correctly.

## Core Principle

Kael is not chosen. She becomes impossible to classify.

## Explanation

Her anomalous state comes from the interaction of four separate factors: her psychic nature, displacement exposure, later human augmentation, and the Unity event involving Erit. No single factor makes her an anomaly — it's their overlap.

After augmentation, Kael no longer fits the normal resolution pathway. Her condition doesn't resolve as expected, because Qalm no longer tracks her correctly. This is the mechanism that later makes Unity possible at all — the cycle can only break somewhere it's already stopped classifying cleanly.
